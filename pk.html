Punjab Kings

Punjab Kings is a franchise cricket team in the Indian Premier League, based in Mohali, Punjab. Established in 2008, the team was formerly known as Kings XI Punjab before rebranding as Punjab Kings in 2021. Owned by a group including Preity Zinta, Ness Wadia, and Mohit Burman, the team plays its home matches at the PCA Stadium.

In the IPL 2025 auction, Punjab Kings acquired Shreyas Iyer for a record Rs 26.75 crore, Arshdeep Singh for Rs 18 crore, and Yuzvendra Chahal for Rs 18 crore. They also secured Marcus Stoinis (Rs 11 crore) and Glenn Maxwell (Rs 4.2 crore). Additionally, they bought Nehal Wadhera, Harpreet Brar, Vishnu Vinod, Vijaykumar Vyshak, and Yash Thakur.
