Kolkata Knight Riders

Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) is a franchise cricket team in the Indian Premier League (IPL), representing the city of Kolkata. They have won the IPL three times, in 2012, 2014, and 2024. The team is owned by a consortium including Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan, actress Juhi Chawla, and her husband Jay Mehta. Their home ground is Eden Gardens, a historic stadium in Kolkata.

They became the IPL champions in 2012, by defeating Chennai Super Kings in the final. They repeated the feat in 2014, defeating Kings XI Punjab. In 2024, they won the title for the third time by beating Sunrisers Hyderabad. The Knight Riders hold the record for the longest winning streak by any IPL team (14).
