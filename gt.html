Gujarat Titans

Founded in 2021, Gujarat Titans' home ground is Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad. The franchise is owned by Torrent Group and CVC Capital Partners. The team is currently captained by Shubman Gill. The Gujarat Titans are coached by Ashish Nehra.

Gujarat Titans had one of the best all-round squads in the previous cycle, reaching the final in their first two seasons as a franchise and showing how they had built an efficient and well-rounded unit that capitalised on star power and terrific role players alike.
