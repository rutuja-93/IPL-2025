Chennai Super King

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is an Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. They are known for their strong fanbase and have won five IPL titles. CSK was one of the eight original franchises in the IPL.

History:

CSK was one of the original eight franchises in the IPL when the league was established in 2008.

Ownership:

The team is owned by Chennai Super Kings Cricket Limited, a subsidiary of India Cements.

Success:

CSK has won the IPL five times (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023) and also won the Champions League Twenty20 twice (2010, 2014).

Leadership:

MS Dhoni was the captain from 2008 to 2023. Currently, Ruturaj Gaikwad is the captain, and Stephen Fleming is the coach.

Home Ground:

CSK plays their home games at the M. A. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai.

Recent News:

In 2025, CSK finished in fifth place in the previous season. MS Dhoni took over as captain in April 2025 after Ruturaj Gaikwad withdrew due to an elbow injury.
