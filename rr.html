Rajastan Royals

Rajasthan Royals (often abbreviated as RR) are a professional franchise cricket team based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, that competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008 as one of the initial eight IPL franchises, the team is owned by Manoj Badale and The Royals Sports Group.

Rajasthan Royals won the inaugural edition of IPL in 2008. They have made the playoffs six times, including the final in IPL 2022. The Indian Premier League has grown to be one of the most popular leagues in the world since its inaugural edition in 2008.
