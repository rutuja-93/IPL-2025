Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB), a franchise in the Indian Premier League (IPL), is based in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Owned by United Spirits, the team was founded in 2008 and is named after the liquor brand "Royal Challenge". RCB has been a consistent participant in the IPL, reaching the final three times (2009, 2011, and 2016) but has yet to win a title.
