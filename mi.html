Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians is a cricket team in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team is owned by Reliance Industries. They have won the IPL five times, the most of any team. The team is based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, and plays its home games at Wankhede Stadium.

Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB), a franchise in the Indian Premier League (IPL), is based in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Owned by United Spirits, the team was founded in 2008 and is named after the liquor brand "Royal Challenge". RCB has been a consistent participant in the IPL, reaching the final three times (2009, 2011, and 2016) but has yet to win a title.

IPL Title:

Mumbai Indians has won the IPL title five times, in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, and 2020.

Ownership:

Reliance Industries owns the team through its subsidiary, IndiaWin Sports.

Coaching Staff:

The team's coaching staff includes Mark Boucher as head coach, Jonty Rhodes as fielding coach, and Shaun Pollock as bowling coach.

Captain:

Rohit Sharma is the current captain of the team.

Home Venue:

The team plays its home games at the Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai.
